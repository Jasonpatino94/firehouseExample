About Us

East Windsor Volunteer Company was Founded in 1970(Check this to make sure its true). Located at 69 Twin Rivers Drive, East Windsor, NJ by the Ethel Mcknight Elementary school in Twin Rivers you will find us training, working, or even just hanging out. We host annual Halloween parties for the kids and cover the annual fireworks at Etra Park Lake. We are 100% Volunteer EWVFC2 is always searching for dedicated and committed volunteers to join us. We offer free training, knowledge, some of the best friendship you'll ever have, and awesome memories that youll never forget..plus more!! Click here and find out how to join us!
